A Modern Vision for a Historic Space

The Diamond Jubilee Hall holds the memories of thousands of graduates. As our institution grows, our facilities must evolve to meet world-class standards.

This comprehensive renovation project goes beyond aesthetics. We are completely overhauling the infrastructure to transform the hall into a premier, climate-controlled venue capable of hosting international conferences, cultural fests, and high-profile alumni events.

  • Centralized Air Conditioning: Complete climate control for year-round comfort.
  • Acoustic Overhaul: State-of-the-art soundproofing and audio systems.
  • Ancillary Spaces: New VIP lounges, modernized green rooms, and upgraded backstage logistics.
  • Sustainable Upgrades: Energy-efficient LED lighting and eco-friendly seating materials.
